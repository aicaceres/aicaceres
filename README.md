**Software Engineer → AI Product Manager in training**

Throughout my career, **code was always the means, not the end**. What's driven me is understanding the problem, designing the solution, and seeing it through until it actually works for real users.

I'm currently studying the **AI Product Manager** career at EducaciónIT — integrating Design Thinking, OKR, Product Discovery, and AI-driven product development into my technical foundation. In my current role at Verifarma, I already work with user stories in Jira, participate in refinements, and collaborate on requirements definition.

My edge: I speak both languages — **engineering and business** — and can bridge dev teams and stakeholders without losing credibility on either side.
